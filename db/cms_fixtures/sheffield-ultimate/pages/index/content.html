Welcome! Steal is a Sheffield's [Ultimate Frisbee][uf] club. We [train regularly][t] together and [compete][r] in Mixed and Open Tour as well as fun tournaments throughout the year. While our main aim is to finish well at Tour, we love to play Ultimate and have a lot of fun. If you've recently started playing or have never thrown a disc before, come along to one of our [training sessions][t] and join in! Our captains are [UK Ultimate approved coaches][ukul1] and the club has an abundance of experienced, welcoming players that will help improve your game.
